Rational

You are being asked to help The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (UNC) conduct research on the effectiveness of text simplification. This will be used to help develop a website that will simplify texts. Our goal is to improve the way we simplify text to make complicated books and articles easier to understand.

Background

You will read or listen to texts and decide which are easy and which are hard. This should take no longer than 30 minutes to complete. This is completely optional and not doing it will not affect your relationship with UNC. If you for any reason do not want to finish, you are welcome to stop.

Disclaimer

We will be using the information you provide to measure the quality of simplification from our Text Simplification Model. To protect your identity, we will NOT store any identifiable information that you record on this quiz. We take any potential security risks very seriously and will take measures to ensure any stored data is secure.
